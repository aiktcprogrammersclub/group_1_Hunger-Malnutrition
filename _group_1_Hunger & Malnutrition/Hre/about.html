Who We are

is a global humanitarian organization committed to ending world hunger and undernourishment, works to save the lives of malnourished children while providing communities with access to safe water and sustainable solutions to hunger.

What We do

We have been working in 23 countries for the past decade benefiting over a 1,200,000 people.We have over 5000 plus aid workers and volunteers in these countries to ensure that world hunger is reduced in a sustainable and economical manner. We seek to provide our resources to everyone who needs it. We are a trusted organisation and have received the highest four-star rating from Charity Navigator, seven years in a row. But none of this would be possible without the support of our donors and volunteers. World hunger is the problem, and we seek to be the solution. Join us !
